Shrimp and Soft Tofu Stew

Description

Very easy to make and nutritious, perfect for beginner cooks

Ingredients

  • 1 package of soft tofu, cut into 1 cm cubes
  • 8 pcs of medium sized shrimp, peeled and cut into 1cm bits
  • green onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic, crushed and chopped into small pieces
  • 3 slices of ginger
  • oyster sauce, fish sauce, sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp of corn starch

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp of canola oil on a cooking pan
  2. Add garlic and ginger, stir fry until brown
  3. Add shrimps, stir fry until they turn orange, do not overcook
  4. Add some hot water
  5. Add soft tofu
  6. Season with oyster sauce, fish sauce, salt and pepper
  7. Cook until boiling
  8. Dissolve corn starch in 1/4 cup of water, pour into the cooking and continue cooking for 2-3 mins until the sauce thickens
  9. Add green onions, and a few drops of sesame oil for final touch
