Pork Spareribs Soup with Preserved Vegetable

Description

Comfort food for rainy or cold weather. Very fast and easy to make using InstantPot

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of pork spareribs, cut into smaller pieces
  • 1 packet of preserved vegetable, cut into smaller pieces
  • 1 packet of medium firm tofu, cut into 2 cm cubes
  • 3 cloves of garlic, peel and chop into small pieces
  • 3 slices of ginger
  • 1/4 cup of soysauce

Steps

  1. Marinate spareribs with soysauce 1 hour before cooking
  2. Use Saute function in InstantPot to heat up some canola oil
  3. Stir fry garlic and ginger until brown
  4. Stir fry spareribs until the surface turns brown
  5. Add preserved vegetables and tofu
  6. Add about 1.5-2 liters of water, enough to cover the ingredients
  7. Add some salt and pepper
  8. Put the lid on the InstantPot, put the Steam Release Valve on Seal position
  9. Use "Meat/Stew" function (35 min) on InstantPot
  10. Soup is ready to be enjoyed in 35 mins. Put the Valve on Release position if you're in a hurry
